Welcome to the IBA Chatbot Testing Sandbox

Testing and refining the interactive chatbot for our official university website.

Project Overview

This sandbox environment is designed for the development and testing of the chatbot for IBA Websites. Here, we aim to provide an interactive virtual assistant that helps students, staff, and visitors easily navigate through the website and find important information. The chatbot will assist with inquiries such as admission details, rules and policies, program information, campus events, and much more.

Chatbot Features

  • Quick answers to frequently asked questions
  • Easy navigation to academic and administrative pages
  • Escalating queries to respective departments and officials
  • Enhanced user experience for prospective students and visitors
